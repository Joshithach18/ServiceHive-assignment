# AutoStream Knowledge Base

## What is AutoStream?
AutoStream is a SaaS platform that provides automated video editing tools for content creators. It uses AI to streamline video editing workflows, making it easy for YouTube creators, Instagram influencers, TikTokers, and other content creators to produce professional-quality videos faster.

## Pricing Plans

### Basic Plan — $29/month
- 10 videos per month
- 720p resolution output
- Standard AI editing features
- Business hours support (9 AM – 6 PM EST, Mon–Fri)
- Access to basic templates

### Pro Plan — $79/month
- Unlimited videos per month
- 4K resolution output
- AI captions (auto-generated subtitles)
- Priority 24/7 customer support
- Access to all templates and advanced AI tools
- Team collaboration features (up to 3 seats)

## Company Policies

### Refund Policy
AutoStream does not offer refunds after 7 days from the date of purchase. Refund requests submitted within the first 7 days of a subscription will be reviewed on a case-by-case basis. Contact support@autostream.ai for refund inquiries.

### Support Policy
24/7 customer support is exclusively available to Pro plan subscribers. Basic plan users have access to standard support during business hours (9 AM – 6 PM EST, Monday to Friday).

### Cancellation Policy
You may cancel your subscription at any time. Cancellation takes effect at the end of the current billing cycle. No partial refunds are given for unused periods.

### Data Privacy
AutoStream stores your uploaded video data securely. Videos are automatically deleted from our servers 30 days after processing unless you choose to keep them in your library.

### Free Trial
We offer a 7-day free trial on the Pro plan. No credit card required to start the trial.

## Getting Started
To get started with AutoStream:
1. Choose a plan (Basic or Pro)
2. Create your account with your email
3. Connect your content platform (YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, etc.)
4. Upload your raw footage and let the AI do the editing

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I upgrade from Basic to Pro?
Yes! You can upgrade at any time. The price difference is prorated for the remainder of your billing cycle.

### Is there a free trial?
We offer a 7-day free trial on the Pro plan. No credit card required to start.

### Which platforms does AutoStream support?
AutoStream supports content creators on YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, Twitch, Facebook, and LinkedIn.

### What video formats are supported?
AutoStream supports MP4, MOV, AVI, MKV, and WebM input formats. Output is delivered as MP4 or MOV.

### Can I cancel anytime?
Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. Your access continues until the end of the billing period.
